## The evidence base

This directory contains curated research organized by domain. Each subdirectory focuses on a specific axis of the convergence. The goal is not comprehensiveness — it's clarity. We prioritize papers, datasets, and results that *changed understanding* over those that merely confirmed it.

<br>

| Directory | Focus | Key Question |

| [scaling-laws/](scaling-laws/) | The mathematics of capability growth | How predictable is progress? |

<br>

## How to contribute

Add a paper or result to the relevant subdirectory. Include a brief note — two or three sentences — explaining why it matters. Not what it says. Why it *shifts understanding.*

Format:

```markdown
## [Paper Title](link)
Authors, Year

Why it matters: [Your brief note on significance]
```

We value quality over quantity. One paradigm-shifting paper is worth more than fifty incremental ones.
